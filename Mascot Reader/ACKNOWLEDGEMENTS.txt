
This node uses the excellent mascotdatfile library (and dependancies)
to do most of the work.  The website for mascotdatfile can be found at:

http://code.google.com/p/mascotdatfile/

and has been published as:
Helsens et al. MascotDatfile: an open-source library to fully 
parse and analyse MASCOT MS/MS search results. Proteomics (2007) vol. 7 (3) 
pp. 364-6 (pid:17203510)
